Aims and Scope

Journal of Infectious Diseases and Public Health is an open access peer-reviewed journal from Iatric Publications which intended to publish original research, review, case studies, opinion, commentary, book-reviews, image and editorials on clinical and laboratory research on infectious diseases, microbiology, immunology, epidemiology, mental health, infections, vaccines, disease transmissions, zoonoses and practices in public health.
