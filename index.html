Journal of Infectious Diseases and Public Health is an open access peer-reviewed journal from Iatric Publications which intended to publish original research, review, case studies, opinion, commentary, book-reviews, clinical images and editorials on clinical and laboratory research on infectious diseases, microbiology, immunology, epidemiology, mental health, infections, vaccines, disease transmissions, zoonoses and practices in public health.

Journal of Infectious Diseases and Public Health believes in publishing scholarly articles receiving from world-wide with Quality, Research integrity, Publication ethics, and Promoting Innovation. Our aim is to publish the latest information and research related to infection outbreaks, disease transmissions, and public health which helps to reduce the risk outcomes of the infections.
