Journal of Infectious Diseases and Public Health is an open access peer-reviewed journal from Iatric Publications considers papers which are directly submitted to Editorial Office. After the successful recipient of manuscript a unique Manuscript number will be allotted to the corresponding author within 48 hours of the submission. Editorial Office will take care of the publication process and will update you the status of the paper frequently. In general, Time span for review process is 21 days. Time span to process a paper from Submission to Publication is 28 days. Accepted paper will be available online with in 7days after acceptance.
